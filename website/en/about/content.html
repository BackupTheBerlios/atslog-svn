ATSlog gives a convenient interface with access from the web-browser for wathing and analysis of calls of the different models of PBX. The program is free, it spreads under GPL license, and it has fully opened code.
The program is successfully working with such models as Panasonic KX-TA308, KX-TA308RU, KX-TA616RU, KX-TD816RU, KX-TD1232; Samsung SKP-816. If you want to put into the register of supported by PBX another model, you should look to the division <The upgrading and extension of the program>.

An author will be thankful to the programmer, who will help the development of the program. The information is in division <For programmer>

The abilities of ATSlog

  1. A comfortable web-interface for access to the statistics:
    • Flexible mode of the task of a range of dates.
    • An opportunity to analyze the information from two sides — either from the side of the outside telephone lines that are connected to the ATS or from the side of internal telephones.
    • The separating of the call statistics on different types — the in-town calls, the calls between cities and countries, incoming and outcoming.
    • A color painting for different types of calls.
    • A sorting by the head-lines of the columns of the table (direct and indirect order).
    • Before printing preparing.
    • Cashing of the SQL asks, which allows quicker working on the information.
    • To watch the screenshots.
    • A test watching of the statistics. You can get in the real using of the program ATSlog. To enter, you should use the name of the user 'atslog', password 'atslog'
  2. The server functions :
    • The shift of text log-files ATS into the format of the program ATSlog.
    • A rotation of the text log-files with the information of the telephone calls of PBX.
    • Critical messages about the mistakes are shifted in the syslog.

The model of the program

  • For work you need: SH, Perl 5.X, PHP 4.X. Everything was tested on the base of Apache server 1.3.X
  • The program wokr on *NIX platforms: FreeBSD, Linux; The porting under other POSIX systems is possible.
  • To work the module atslogd is used for reading the information from ATS, perl script is used for writing in the data base, PHP and any http server is used for access to the statistics throughout the web-interface.
  • ATSlog is easily used and tuned.
